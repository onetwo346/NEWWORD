XOwars: Cosmic Battle

Welcome to the ultimate tic-tac-toe showdown in the stars! Choose your mode: battle a friend locally, challenge a galactic AI, or connect across the cosmos with a pin code for online play. Align three symbols (X or O) to claim victory in this interstellar arena.

How to Play:

  1. Pick your cosmic colors for X and O.
  2. Launch into the game with "Start Battle."
  3. Select your mode: Local, AI, or Online Multiplayer.
  4. For online play, generate a pin code or join a friend’s game.
  5. Take turns until a victor emerges or the cosmos declares a draw.
