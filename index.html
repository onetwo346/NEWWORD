XOwars

Welcome to XOwars, a fun tic-tac-toe game! Play with a friend on the same device, challenge the computer, or connect online with a friend using a game code.

How to Play:

  1. Choose colors for Player X and Player O.
  2. Click "Start Game" to begin.
  3. Pick your mode: Local, Computer, or Online.
  4. For online play, one player creates a game code, and the other joins using that code.
  5. Take turns to get three symbols in a row and win!
