Welcome to XOwars!

XOwars is a classic tic-tac-toe game with three exciting modes: battle a friend locally, challenge our smart AI, or play online with a friend anywhere in the world using a pin code! Get three of your symbols (X or O) in a row—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally—to win.

How to Play:

  1. Choose your colors for Player X and Player O.
  2. Click "Start Game" to begin.
  3. Select a mode: Two-Player (local), AI, or Online Multiplayer.
  4. For online play, generate a pin code or join with a friend’s code.
  5. Take turns placing your symbol until someone wins or it’s a draw.
