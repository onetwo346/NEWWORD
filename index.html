XOwars

Welcome to XOwars, a fun tic-tac-toe game! Play with a friend on the same device, challenge the computer, or connect with a friend online using game codes.

How to Play:

  1. Choose colors for Player X and Player O.
  2. Click "Start Game" to begin.
  3. Pick your mode: Local, Computer, or Online.
  4. For online play, generate a game code, share it with a friend, and enter their code to connect.
  5. Take turns to get three symbols in a row and win!
